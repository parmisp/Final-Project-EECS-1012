Our project, Rate My Dorm (Ontario), is a platform where students can share reviews and rate their dorms that they have lived in. The goal is to help other students find the best dorm for their needs and lifestyles. Students can give feedback on things like cleanliness, facilities, noise levels, how social the dorm is, and their overall experience.
